PROJECT: Enterprise-Level Website Design for "Hello Puncture"

OBJECTIVE:
Design a modern, enterprise-grade service website for "Hello Puncture", a professional doorstep vehicle service brand. The website must convey speed, reliability, technical expertise, and professionalism. The final output should look scalable, trustworthy, and suitable for a city-wide or multi-city service operation.

BRAND IDENTITY:
Brand Name: Hello Puncture
Logo: Use white version of the logo as the primary logo
Tagline: One Call Service | Your Problems, Our Solutions

COLOR PALETTE (STRICT):
Primary Color: Yellow (main highlight color)
Secondary Color: Black (authority, structure, backgrounds)
Neutral: White (logo, text clarity, spacing)
Avoid gradients, pastel tones, neon colors, or soft shadows.

Color Usage Rules:
- Yellow for CTAs, icons, pricing highlights, section dividers
- Black for headers, footer, hero background, strong contrast sections
- White for typography, spacing, and logo dominance
- Maintain high contrast for enterprise-level readability

DESIGN STYLE:
- Enterprise-grade, clean, structured layout
- Strong grid system and alignment
- Bold typography with clear hierarchy
- Minimal, purposeful animations only
- Mobile-first and fully responsive
- Premium service brand appearance (not a local flyer style)

HEADER (ENTERPRISE STYLE):
- Sticky header
- Left: White logo
- Right: Navigation menu
  Home
  Services
  Bike Servicing Packages
